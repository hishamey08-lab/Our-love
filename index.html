✦ How It All Began ✦

Our Story

The Very First Day — 8th Grade 🌸

A Pen That Changed Everything 🖊️

A new school year, new faces in class. Laila ma'am had just finished taking names when Rishal asked Hisham for a pen. No one had one — until Rasla did. The moment Hisham saw her, something stirred in his heart. He gave his own pen to Rishal and kept hers. That little pen became his most treasured thing.

🖊️
💬

A Few Days Later

The First Proposal 💬

Hisham's friends teased him, but he didn't deny it — he pressed that pen to his chest and said "Yes, I love her." Then he went and told her. She said no. Her heart belonged to someone else. But Hisham didn't walk away — he stayed, he supported her, he cared for her quietly, without asking for anything in return.

9th Grade — Three Months In 💗

She Liked Him Back 💗

A whole year had passed. They were in 9th grade now. Then one day, her friends came to Hisham with a secret — Rasla liked him. He smiled and said he still felt the same. They met, they talked, they sorted everything out — and just like that, a beautiful love story officially began.

💗
💑

July 22, 2024 — Forever 💑

Us, Always 💑

From that moment forward, Hisham loved Rasla and Rasla loved Hisham. What started with a borrowed pen grew into something deep, real, and unshakeable. They would do anything for each other — and that love only keeps growing stronger every single day.
